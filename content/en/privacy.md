This site uses **Google Analytics 4** to understand how visitors use the site. Analytics data is anonymised — IP addresses are not stored. You can opt out by enabling **Do Not Track** in your browser or using the [Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).
